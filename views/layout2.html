About Us

E-Vote is a software as a service platform for online voting. Elections managed using E-Vote as anonymous, and verifiable. The system allows voters to check that their vote has been correctly recorded and counted. It prevents managers and system administrators from tampering with the system. It allow observers to independntly recount the election.
